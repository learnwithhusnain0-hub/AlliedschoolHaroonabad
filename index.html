About Our School

Welcome to Allied School Haroonabad

Established in 1995, Allied School Haroonabad has been a beacon of excellence in education, nurturing young minds for over 25 years. Our commitment to holistic development sets us apart.

We believe in creating an environment where students are encouraged to explore, discover, and excel in both academic and extracurricular activities.

Our Vision

To be the premier educational institution that develops innovative, compassionate, and globally competitive citizens.

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating learning environment that empowers students to become lifelong learners and responsible leaders.

Principal's Message

"At Allied School, we believe in nurturing minds and building characters that will shape the future of our nation."

It is with great pride and pleasure that I welcome you to Allied School Haroonabad. Our institution is built on the foundation of academic excellence, moral values, and holistic development.

We understand that each child is unique, with their own strengths and talents. Our dedicated faculty works tirelessly to provide personalized attention to help every student reach their full potential.

Our curriculum is designed not only to impart knowledge but also to develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. We prepare our students to face the challenges of the modern world with confidence and resilience.

Pre-School Program

Where learning begins with fun and exploration

A Special Place for Little Learners

Our Pre-School program is designed to provide a warm, nurturing environment where young children can take their first steps into the world of learning.

Learning Through Play

We believe that play is the work of childhood. Our play-based curriculum helps children develop cognitive, social, emotional, and physical skills naturally and joyfully.

Admissions

Begin your child's educational journey with us

Admission Process

We have a straightforward admission process designed to make it easy for parents to enroll their children.

1

Inquiry & Visit

Contact us to schedule a school tour and learn about our programs. Visit our campus to see our facilities and meet our staff.

2

Application Submission

Complete the admission application form available at our office or download it from our website.

3

Document Submission

Submit required documents including birth certificate, previous school records, and photographs.

4

Assessment & Interview

For certain grades, students may be assessed to determine appropriate placement and support needs.

5

Admission Confirmation

Receive admission confirmation and fee payment details to complete the enrollment process.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Copy)
  • Previous School Report Card (If applicable)
  • 4 Recent Passport-sized Photographs
  • B-Form / Child's CNIC (If available)
  • Parent's CNIC (Copy)
  • Vaccination Record (For Pre-School)
  • Transfer Certificate (For Grade 2 onwards)
  • Character Certificate (From previous school)
